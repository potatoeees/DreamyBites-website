DELIVERY:

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we do provide delivery services up to a certain distance from our bakery. Additional charges may apply based on the delivery distance.

What is the delivery radius and cost?

We deliver to areas within 50km of our shop. The delivery charges range from RM9 to RM40, depending on the distance. The delivery cost will be calculated based on the distance and will be provided to you when you place your order.

Can I schedule a specific delivery time?

Yes, you can request a specific delivery time during the ordering process. We will do our best to accommodate your preferred time, but please note that delivery slots are subject to availability.
